Location

National Institute of Technology Puducherry (NITPY) is situated in Karaikal. NITPY is located on the National Highway NH32 at about 58 Kms from Chidambaram towards South. The Institute is located about 8.5 Kms from Karaikal Bus stand on the Nagapattinam to Chennai Highway (NH 32). Karaikal Railway Station is about 11 Kms from the NITPY. The simplest and most economical way to reach NITPY is Bus Routes from Puducherry and Chennai pass through the Institute (to stop at Thiruvettakudy Arch) to reach Karaikal Bus Stand.
